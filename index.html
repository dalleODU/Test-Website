Our Problem Statement

Many young professionals in the U.S. struggle with their finances due to the overwhelming burden of student loans, ever increasing credit rates, and a lack of experience with crucial financial skills.

Balancing the pressures of starting a career while facing financial uncertainty leads to the accumulation of debt, increased anxiety, and delayed life milestones such as marriage, home ownership, and starting a family.

CreditTrax aims to empower young professionals with personalized budgeting and debt management tools designed to simplify financial decisions, enhance financial literacy, and set young professionals back on track for financial success.

Problem Characteristics

Financial Literacy

A lack of financial literacy contributes to growing debt and confusion for young professionals, including:

  • Difficulty creating a realistic budget.
  • Uncertainty about where their money is going.
  • Struggles with prioritizing expenses.
  • Lack of understanding about how late payments affect their financial future.

A Lack of Options

A Lack of well-rounded financial options results in issues such as:

  • Relying on multiple seperate financial apps to manage finances.
  • Increased difficulty staying organized, making financial management harder than it should be.
  • A blurred vision of long-term financial goals.

Forgotten Payments

Forgotten monthly payments can lead to accumulating debt and penalites, including:

  • Rising interest rates.
  • Late fees and penalties.
  • A declining credit score.
    • Difficulty making significant purchases.
  • Additional time needed to pay off debt.

The Solution: CreditTrax

CreditTrax leverages AI-powered budgeting through personalized financial dashboards and interactive tools to help users align their spending with long-term financial goals while making smarter financial decisions.

By analyzing spending habits, sending real-time notifications, and offering comprehensive financial literacy tools, CreditTrax enables young professionals to achieve their financial objectives, track their progress, and reduce the stress and anxiety that often comes with managing debt.

How CreditTrax Will Help

Debt Tracking

CreditTrax provides users with a clear, organized view of their financial obligations

  • Input Debts: Add credit card balances, student loans, their interest rates, and due dates.
  • Smart Payment Reminders: Manual payment prompts with increasing reminders to ensure timely payments.

Budgeting Recommendations

CreditTrax provides helpful suggestions and tips to keep everything on track!

  • AI-Powered Advice: Personalized advice based on debt and spending habits.
  • Tailored Suggestions: Analyzes income, expenses, and debt obligations for optimized budgeting.
  • Smart Spending Helps reduce unnecessary expenses and allows users to allocate funds more efficiently.

Personal Finance Dashboard

Helpful suggestions and tips to keep everything on track!

  • Data Analytics: Tracks spending, debt obligations, and income flow to provide real-time financial health reports.
  • Visualize Your Progress: Uses charts, graphs, and badge rewards system to visualize and reward user progress.
  • Smart Spending Helps reduce unnecessary expenses and allows users to allocate funds more efficiently.
